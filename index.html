Meet the four

The low earner


Meet Neche. She works hard at a filling station, earning NGN 25,000 monthly. But like many of us, managing money can be a challenge. Without careful planning, she might find herself running out of cash before the month ends. That’s where My Purse comes in. With My Purse, Neche can access interest-free overdrafts, giving her a little extra to spend every month — with zero fees attached. Yes, you heard right. Zero fees. My Purse is here to help you stretch your income without the stress.

The saver


Meet Kenneth. As a video editor, Kenneth needs a new MacBook to level up his work. But saving up for it could take a few months, and the process can feel overwhelming. That’s where My Purse steps in. With My Purse, Kenneth can easily save towards his goal, making the journey stress-free and seamless. In no time, he’ll walk confidently into an Apple store and walk out with his dream MacBook. Big goals, made simple.

The parent


Meet Mrs. Okpala. Her son, let’s just say, isn’t the best with money. Every month, she sends him NGN 40,000 for school expenses, but somehow, it’s gone before the month is halfway through — spent on things he doesn’t even need. Enter My Purse. Now, Mrs. Okpala uses My Purse to budget his allowance and auto-send it to him in smaller, manageable portions throughout the month. It’s the perfect way to teach him financial discipline while ensuring his needs are met. Smart money habits start here.

The student


Meet Victor. As a university student, Victor knows the struggle of being broke all too well — sometimes he can’t even afford data. But that’s all in the past, thanks to My Purse. With My Purse, Victor can now budget his money with ease and access overdrafts when he needs a little extra. Not to brag, but we’re every student’s best friend.
